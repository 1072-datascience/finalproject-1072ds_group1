申請流程
1.      英文成績單*1
2.      推薦信*2
3.      短文(essay)*3
4.      履歷*1
5.      電話面試*2
*需要5年以上工作經驗，另外如果公司主要語言不是英文的話還是需要考GRE iBT

另外關於短文
這個部分真的花了很多時間
幾乎兩個月都在準備
總共需要寫三篇
1.      Discuss your objectives for pursuing this degree. Why do you want to
obtain a Master of Science in Business Analytics from NYU Stern at this point
in your career?
2.      The NYU Stern MS in Business Analytics program is a unique degree
offering a curriculum at the intersection of business strategy and data
science.
Explain a situation in which the integration of these skills could have been
better utilized to provide a more successful business outcome.
3.      How do you see yourself contributing to the MSBA program?
Specifically, what value will you bring as a member of the cohort given your
previous educational, professional and life experiences?

第一篇我稍微提了一下以前在漁港大學在船上實習的經驗，跟我在漁港大學做研究，然後
後來工作每天都需要用到分析。
